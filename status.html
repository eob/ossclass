Status Updates

Weekly status updates are designed to get you into the habit of planning, scoping, evaluating, and reflecting upon your work in the way that you will be expected to in industry. Each week, by midnight the day before your group meeting, each group should create a Google Doc in your group folder that answers the following questions:

  • What did you accomplish last week?
  • What new information do you have?
  • What are you planning for this week?
  • What is blocking you?
