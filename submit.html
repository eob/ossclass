Submitting Materials

We're going to try using the Google (GDocs, G+) ecosystem for materials submission for this class.

  • Group Status Updates: Create a new Google Document in your group folder in GDocs. Include the date in the document name. I.e., "Status Update 01-29-2013".
  • Developer Journal: Post to Google+ and share it just with edward.benson@gmail.com. (You're free to share with anyone you like, but you're only required to share with me.)
