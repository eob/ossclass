Development Journal

Goal. Develop a practice of reflecting on areas of software development and your work.

Guidelines. Write as you would write an email or a memo to a colleague. That is: don't write an epic novel, but don't write a bullet-list of sentence fragments. Feel free to write a lot if the mood strikes you, but a few (2-3) paragraphs to a page is adequate.

We'll often be providing you with a question, and sometimes a short piece of reading material to react to. In your reaction, we encourage you to think about big picture ideas in addition to the specifics of the question at hand.
