Quantifying your Project

Through measurement and reflection, we develop an understanding of how we might improve things.

Propose three different metrics to measure on your code-base that would help generate a better understanding of how to improve the architecture. You might be measuring time, the count of something, or maybe the the distribution of parameters under which something occurs. For each proposal, explain what the metric is, how it relates to the project, what you might learn from measuring it, and what you might do with that information.
